Love is a funny thing.

Typically you cannot see love.

But when you can it is very clear.

Some people think love is black and white, others see it as white and black.

There are many defintions of love. According to the Merriam Webster's Dictionary there are at least 8 definitions of love. Here are the ones I find most important.

  1. A strong affection for another arising out of kinship or personal ties.
  2. An attraction based on sexual desire : affection and tenderness felt by lovers.
  3. An affection based on admiration, benevolence, or common interests.
  4. A warm attachment, enthusiasm, or devotion.
  5. The object of attachment, devotion, or admiration.
  6. A score of zero (as in tennis).
